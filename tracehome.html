TraceHome

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026  ·  Developer: Miguel P. Pais / Clarityscape

TraceHome does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account is required to use the app.

What this app does

TraceHome is an offline-first app that tracks home inventory (purchase price, condition, photos) and stores it entirely on your device. It allows you to generate PDF and CSV reports of your inventory locally.

Data we collect

None. TraceHome works entirely on your device. No analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising SDKs are included.

Currency Exchange Rates

The app requires internet access to fetch live exchange rates from the Frankfurter API (api.frankfurter.app). No user data, personally identifiable information (PII), or inventory data is sent in these requests.

AI Image Processing

We implemented On-Device ML Object Recognition using Google ML Kit. All image processing happens 100% locally on your device's hardware. Photos of your home are NEVER uploaded to any external server.

Device permissions

  • Camera and Photos — used to capture or select images of your inventory items. Images are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted.
  • Storage — used to save item data locally in a database and to generate and save PDF or CSV reports.

Cloud backups

We rely on the native OS auto-backup systems (Apple iCloud and Google Drive). We do not host, store, or transmit the user's database to our own servers. If you have cloud backup enabled on your device, your operating system may back up this data as part of its normal app backup process:

  • Android: Google may back up app data to your Google Drive account via Android Auto Backup, subject to your Google account settings.
  • iOS: Apple may sync app settings and files to your iCloud account, subject to your iCloud settings.

This backup is handled entirely by your device's operating system and your own cloud account. Clarityscape has no access to this data.

Children's privacy

TraceHome does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
