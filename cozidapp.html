CozidApp

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026  ·  Developer: Miguel P. Pais / Clarityscape

CozidApp does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account is required to use the app.

What this app does

CozidApp helps you find restaurants serving Cozido à Portuguesa near you, today. It displays restaurant locations on a map, shows opening hours and serving days, and can send you proximity-based alerts during meal times when a cozido restaurant is nearby.

Data we collect

None. CozidApp does not include any analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services. All app preferences (language, theme, alert radius, and notification times) are stored locally on your device only.

Device permissions

  • Location (fine and coarse): Used to show your position on the map and to calculate the distance between you and nearby restaurants. Location is only used on-device and is never transmitted.
  • Internet: Used to load map tiles from OpenStreetMap and to download the restaurant database. No personal information is included in these requests.

Restaurant data

The restaurant database is a curated list maintained by Clarityscape. It contains only publicly available information (restaurant names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and serving days). No user-submitted data is stored on your device beyond your own app preferences.

Cloud backup

CozidApp stores only your personal preferences (not restaurant data or any personal information). If device-level backup is enabled, your operating system (Android or iOS) may back up these preferences through its standard mechanisms (Google Drive or iCloud). Clarityscape has no access to this data.

Children's privacy

CozidApp does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
