RGBot

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026  ·  Developer: Miguel P. Pais / Clarityscape

RGBot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to Clarityscape. No account is required to use the app.

What this app does

RGBot is a real-time color identification tool designed to help people with color vision deficiencies. It uses your device's camera to analyze colors in the scene and displays the color name along with its RGB, Hex, and CMYK values. You can save identified colors to a personal palette and share them. All color processing happens on your device.

Data we collect

Clarityscape does not collect any personal data from RGBot. The app contains no analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising SDKs. The only network activity is the optional one-time in-app purchase described below, which is handled entirely by your device's app store.

Device permissions

  • Camera — used to capture a live video feed for real-time color analysis. Frames are processed locally on your device and are never recorded, stored externally, or transmitted.
  • Flashlight / Torch — used to illuminate subjects in low-light conditions to improve color accuracy. The torch is only activated when you explicitly toggle it within the app.
  • Internet access — required solely to process and verify the optional RGBot Pro in-app purchase through Google Play or the Apple App Store. The app does not connect to any Clarityscape servers.

In-App Purchases

RGBot offers a one-time purchase called RGBot Pro that unlocks additional features. Purchases are processed and verified entirely by Google Play (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS) using the platform's standard billing system. Clarityscape never receives or stores your payment information.

Once your purchase is confirmed by the platform, the Pro status is saved locally on your device. Restoring a purchase on a new device follows the same platform-verified flow and does not involve any Clarityscape servers.

For questions about billing, refunds, or payment history, please refer to your app store account settings:

  • Android: Google Play purchase history & refunds
  • iOS: Apple App Store request a refund

Cloud backup

Your saved color palette and Pro purchase status are stored locally on your device using standard app storage. If you have cloud backup enabled on your device, your operating system may back up this data as part of its normal app backup process:

  • Android: Google may back up app data to your Google Drive account via Android Auto Backup, subject to your Google account settings.
  • iOS: Apple may sync app settings to your iCloud account via iCloud Key-Value Storage, subject to your iCloud settings.

This backup is handled entirely by your device's operating system and your own cloud account. Clarityscape has no access to this data.

Children's privacy

RGBot does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
